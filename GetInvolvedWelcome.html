Get Involved with EPP


It is our desire to foster a community of active EPP users that make suggestions, find errors and in general. We are already indebted to many of our users for sending us excellent suggestions. Many such suggestions have been incorporated into this and other product releases.

For those who are interested in getting involved, please let us know. We would love to have your help! The participate link lists only a few ideas, but many more forms of synergy are possible. Please do not hesitate to contact us.

Thank you!
